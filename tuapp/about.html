Taylor's Mobile

Taylor's Mobile is your pocket companion for all things Taylor's*. An easy-to-use app that's lite on your pocket that puts information on your fingertips, featuring essential information and tools.

Designed to cater for the needs of prospective students, general public and students currently enrolled in Taylor’s University and Taylor’s College, it is an all-in-one app that’s owned and managed by Taylor’s Education Group.

If you have any feedback on the App, please rate us and leave a comment under the “Rate This App” section. If you have problems using the App, Please email us at taylorsmobile@taylors.edu.my.

* Currently the Taylor’s Mobile focuses on Taylor’s higher education institutions, specifically Taylor’s University and Taylor’s Colleges.
